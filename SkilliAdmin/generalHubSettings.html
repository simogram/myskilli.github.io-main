Important information about your learning hub

Published/Draft/Coming Soon

When your Learning Hub is in draft mode, it is totally inaccessible to visitors and students and they wont know it exists. When your Learning Hub is in “Waiting List Mode”, it can be seen by your site visitors but they can only join a waiting list. Select the coming soon mode only when you have set up your Learning Hub Information pages. When your Learning Hub is published, your site visitors can enrol and access its content. Select this mode only after you have completed your Learning Hub setup.
