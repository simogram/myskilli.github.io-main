Published/Draft/Coming Soon

When your course is in draft mode, it is totally inaccessible to visitors and students and they wont know it exists. When your course is in “Coming Soon Mode”, it can be seen by your site visitors but they can only join a waiting list. Select the coming soon mode only when you have set up your course pages. When your course is published, your site visitors can enrol and access the classroom. Select this mode only after you have completed your course setup.
